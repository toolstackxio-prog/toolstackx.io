How to Use

1. Enter Text: Type or paste your desired text into the text box.

2. Customize: Select your preferred handwriting font, ink color, and paper style from the available options.

3. Preview: See a live preview of your text in the handwriting style on the right.

4. Copy or Download: Use the "Copy Text" button to copy the plain text to your clipboard, or click "Download as Image" to save the handwritten note as a PNG file.
