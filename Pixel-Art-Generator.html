  1. **Set the Grid Size**: Enter your desired grid size (e.g., **30**) in the **Grid Size** input.

  6. **Clear Canvas**: Click the **Clear Canvas** button to instantly clear all colors from the current grid.
  7. **Export as PNG**: Click the **Export as PNG** button to download a high-resolution image of your pixel art.
